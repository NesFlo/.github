# NesFlo

Turn Conversations Into Workflows.

NesFlo is a workflow intelligence platform that transforms operational conversations from messaging platforms, emails, forms, APIs, and spreadsheets into structured data, automated workflows, reports, dashboards, and enterprise integrations.

## Our Mission

To bridge the gap between business communication and enterprise systems by eliminating repetitive manual data processing.

## Current Status

NesFlo is currently in the research, validation, and architecture phase.

We're exploring ideas, validating workflows, and building the technical foundation for a scalable SaaS platform.
